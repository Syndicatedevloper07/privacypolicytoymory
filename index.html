Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2025
Privacy First: ToyMory is committed to protecting your privacy. All your toy memories are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

ToyMory does not collect any personal information from users. All data including toy photos, descriptions, memories, and any other content you create within the app is stored exclusively on your device.

Device Information

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or any technical information about your device beyond what is necessary for the app to function properly on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since ToyMory stores all data locally on your device:

  • Your toy memories, photos, and descriptions remain private and under your control
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • We cannot access your stored memories or personal content
  • Your data is not used for analytics, marketing, or any other purposes

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system. This includes:

  • Toy photographs and images
  • Memory descriptions and notes
  • App preferences and settings
  • Any other content you create within the app

Data Security

Your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and using appropriate security features like screen locks.

4. Third-Party Services

Advertising

ToyMory displays advertisements to support the free version of the app. These ads are provided by third-party advertising networks and may require internet connectivity. The advertising services may collect limited information for ad personalization and delivery, but this data is not collected or controlled by ToyMory.

Common advertising data may include:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • IP address for general location
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about advertising data collection, please refer to your device's advertising privacy settings and the privacy policies of the advertising networks.

5. Data Sharing

ToyMory does not share any user data with third parties because we do not collect or have access to your personal data. Your memories and content remain exclusively on your device.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data persists until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • You have complete control over your data retention
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device
  • We cannot recover deleted data as we do not store backups

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. Continued use of ToyMory after changes indicates acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

8. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your stored memories anytime within the app
  • Modification: You can edit or update your memories at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual memories or all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data remains accessible to you through the app interface
